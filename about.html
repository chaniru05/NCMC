About Us

Discover the Nalanda College Mathematics Circle

Our Vision

To inspire a deep appreciation for mathematics among students by fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. We envision a community where mathematical thinking becomes a pathway to innovation and academic excellence.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive and engaging environment where students can explore the beauty of mathematics beyond classroom boundaries. Through competitions, workshops, and collaborative learning, we aim to cultivate mathematical talent and help students discover the real-world applications of mathematical concepts.

Our Journey Through Time

2010

The Beginning

Nalanda College Mathematics Circle was established with just 15 founding members who shared a passion for mathematics beyond the curriculum.

2013

First National Competition

Our members participated in their first national mathematics olympiad, bringing home three medals and establishing our presence in the competitive circuit.

2016

Community Outreach

Launched the "Math for All" initiative, extending mathematical education to neighboring schools and communities through volunteer teaching.

2020

Digital Transformation

Successfully transitioned to virtual meetings and competitions during global challenges, expanding our reach and accessibility.

2025

Today

Now a Grade A society with over 1000 members, continuing to innovate and inspire the next generation of mathematical thinkers.
